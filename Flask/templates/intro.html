Introduction to Project


China, India, and United States are among the countries of the world most affected by natural disasters

Natural disasters have th epotential to wreck and even end the lives of thoase people, who stand in their way. However, whether or not you are likely to be affected by a natural dsaster highly depends on where in the world you live. The objective of the project is to build a web application to detect the type of disaster. The input is taken from the built in web cam, which in turn is given to the pre-trained model.
The model predicts the type of disaster and displays it on the UI.
